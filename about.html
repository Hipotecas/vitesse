About us

Vision

To be a premier international supplier of high performance analog and mixed-signal semiconductor products delivering best in class growth and profitability.

Mission

We partner with our customers to optimize the engineering solutions that interface the real world of temperature, time, power conversion and signal path.

•Outperform the competition through collaboration in everything we do.

Customer
Focus
